Rama Yantra

The Celestial Coordinator

A uniquely ingenious paired instrument for mapping celestial positions using altitude and azimuth. Two complementary cylindrical structures with central pillars feature alternating solid sectors and gaps‚Äî where one has a marked sector, the other has an opening‚Äîenabling continuous 360-degree tracking of any celestial object without blind spots.

Samrat Yantra

The Celestial Coordinator

The world's largest stone sundial and masterpiece of Jantar Mantar, the Samrat Yantra measured solar time to within two seconds. Its massive triangular gnomon aligns parallel to Earth's axis, pointing at the Pole Star, while its shadow sweeps across marble quadrants‚Äîa physical embodiment of Earth's celestial journey used to track time, solar declination, and celestial positions.
